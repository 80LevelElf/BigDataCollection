What is Bigio used for?

Bigio is a several collections special adapted to store a lot of elements. Standart List and LinkedList is great choice for the most of cases, but they become useless if you have to work with thousands and millions of elements because of:
1. Ineffective operations because of structure of List and LinkedList
2. Memory overhead of that system collections.
